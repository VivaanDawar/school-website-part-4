Achievements

The school has introduced initiatives such as ‘Education without seams for class 1’ and ‘daily activity period’ where students learn a variety of life, self-defence and sports skills. These initiatives ensure that the school keeps up with the needs of the day, and provides a holistic learning experience to the students.

We Aim To To live in God’s presence Foster the rich values of Christian and cultural heritage Develop habit of prayer, meditation and contemplation. To reach out to the marginalized through community service To work towards adopting new villages to improve the lives of the inhabitants Strive to draw out the full potential of our students To develop and deliver quality education to all students To address the aspirations and abilities of all students To nurture innovation, creativity and technological abilities of all students Nurture and harness leadership ability among all students Develop world class students Encourage active participation in co and extra-curricular activities.
